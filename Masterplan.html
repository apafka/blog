My Moat For The AI Era

I've noticed myself come back to the same question over and over again.

What is my unique value proposition in this time and age — when computers can one-shot everything digital, and robots will soon have the power to move atoms around? I keep circling back to what I really enjoy doing. Using cannabis as a medicinal tool. Going deeper with mushrooms around a campfire in ceremony for a small circle of friends and founders. Kicking it with the guitar and chants. I'm a modern day shaman. I don't say it boastfully. I'm writing it to direct the reader to what I'm trying to imply. It's all soul and energy on planet earth for us, people. Many people in the modern day are not connected to the source. Their heart and the universe. They're disharmonized from the universe. Shamans heal and align you. I can do that. So my dream is to actually do that. To live that lifestyle. To educate on energy and keeping the vibe. I'd be a walking prayer, lol. But exciting. On my land, with my family, with the plants I've grown and blessed. All the while, I want to run a business. My gut is telling me to write and share — maybe even through video — this side of me. The side of energy, spirituality, cannabis, music, healing, mysticism. And I am deeply drawn to the technical side of things too. Building stuff. Which brings me to the next question. I notice I'm pressuring myself. But I do want to build a one-man or small-team million-dollar business. Maybe even 100 million. Maybe even a billion. The aim is to help people and humanity — but I want to sell a product. And then I catch myself. Maybe I'm deceiving myself. Maybe the first way is the real way. The real moat against AI and technical disruption. It's actually healing and guiding people — kind of like a psychologist, but deeper. I feel I'll make this my life's work anyway. And the best way to start is to start writing daily on this. Right now. First thing in the morning: write what I can do and should do to push this forward. Either I write, or I make a video. Then I write about how I make the video. Treat it as craft. Create content as storytelling and copywriting. Don't overthink the money — because that's not the end goal. The end goal is a sanctuary where I can act as an energetic root for people. Actually — no. I'm BSing myself there. Honestly, I want it to be my home. Not that I'm a custodian of it. But on this property, in this home, I could have a place to host guests and ceremonies. And here's one more thing I just realized: I am deeply into technical stuff. Games. Coding. Building. Runescape. Fortnite. Makes me wonder if I could build something like that too. Something as awesome as Valve or Pokemon. Or as simple and powerful as Basecamp with AI. I'll come back to this later. But this is actually a pretty good draft. But I'll ship it!

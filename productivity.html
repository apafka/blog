Bottleneck concept

In manufacturing chain there is always some bottleneck -- something that is keeping the manufacturing line from running the way it should be running.

Sometimes it's at the beginning of the manufacturing chain like we can't get enough raw materials. Sometimes it's at the end, like we don't have enough warehouse space for the finished product. Or the bottleneck is somewhere in the middle.

If you're running a manufacturing company, there's always a bottleneck. Whatever the bottleneck, it's holding everything up. Job number one is to remove that bottleneck and get everything again.

Elon has basically univesilized that concept and looks at every company like its some sort conceptual assembly line and every given week there is a bottleneck. Guaranteed to be one main thing one main bottleneck that is holding people back.

He then micromanages the solution of **that.** He doesn't need to manage anything else because everything else is running better than that and can focus on that.

Once he understands the bottleneck then Elon goes and talks to the line engineers who understand the technical nature of the bottleneck.
